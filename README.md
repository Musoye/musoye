### what I've been building

**backend and systems**

- **Padipay** — voice-controlled payment system that understands Nigerian English. people talk, money moves.
- **TrustLock** — remote workforce auth with full IP/device/location audit trails. built this for a hackathon, won 2nd place.
- **Logistics backend** — 1,200+ deliveries a month, real-time dispatch, sub-second responses on 100K+ records.
- **HelpBot** — AI assistant that reads your PDFs and answers questions about them. cut response time 65% through prompt tuning.

**machine learning and AI**

- **Brain tumor detection** — trained a custom CNN and fine-tuned ResNet on MRI scans. then took it further: built a federated learning framework so hospitals can collaborate on the model without ever sharing patient data. EfficientNetB0 hit 98.5% accuracy. paper currently in review.
- **CropWatch** — transfer learning on CNNs to detect crop disease from leaf photos. farmers point their phone at a leaf, get a diagnosis. two-stage training pipeline, mobile backend.
- **EcoFlow AI** — real-time IoT digital twin on Google Cloud predicting crowd surges and carbon emissions. event-driven architecture, Firestore and BigQuery split stream. top 25 of 2000+ teams in Saudi Arabia.
- **Fraud detection** — ML model that flags suspicious financial transactions. not glamorous, genuinely useful.
- **Facial emotion recognition** — CNN trained to read human faces. happy, sad, angry, surprised. deployed as a web app.
- **GPT-2 fine-tuning** — rebuilt the GPT-2 architecture from scratch in PyTorch, fine-tuned on a custom dataset for sentiment classification. good exercise in understanding how these things actually work under the hood.
